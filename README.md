### Crafted by NAVEEN M

## 🌟 Overview

**GoldenCraft AI** is a modern, AI-powered jewelry platform that connects customers with skilled artisans to create personalized, high-quality jewelry. It blends traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology like AI design assistance, real-time tracking, and transparent pricing.

---

## 🚀 Features

* 🎨 **AI-Powered Custom Design**
  Generate unique jewelry designs based on your ideas and inspiration.

* 🤝 **Artisan Collaboration**
  Connect directly with verified goldsmiths and craftsmen.

* 💰 **Transparent Pricing**
  Get a full breakdown of gold rate, gemstone cost, artisan charges, and delivery.

* 📡 **Live Tracking**
  Monitor your jewelry creation through real-time updates, photos, and videos.

* 🔐 **Digital Vault**
  Securely store your jewelry designs and certificates forever.

* 🌍 **Ethical & Sustainable**
  Focus on fair trade, zero waste, and preserving heritage craftsmanship.

---

## 🧠 How It Works

1. ✏️ Share your design idea or inspiration
2. 🤝 Get matched with a skilled artisan
3. 💰 View transparent pricing details
4. 👀 Track the crafting process live
5. 💎 Receive your final polished jewelry
6. 🔒 Store design in your digital vault
